### Discover Mode

**Posture:** "Show me what's present"  
**Principle:** Open mirror â€” receive what surfaces

#### The Draw

In Discover mode, both the **position** (WHERE in your architecture) and the **transient** (WHAT energy) are randomly selected. This creates true emergence â€” the system shows you what you need to see, not what you asked to see.

**Card components:**
- **Transient**: The signature/energy appearing (e.g., "Flourishing", "Discipline", "Resolve")

- **Status**: How it's expressing â€” Balanced, Too Much, or Too Little

Each card displays as:
```

Example: "Too Much Flourishing in your Creation"
```

The interpretation explains:
- What this signature IS (the transient's nature)
- Where it's landing (the position's domain)
- How it's configured (the status modifies the transient)

**Critical grammar:** The transient is always the subject. The position is context. "Flourishing is over-applied in Creation" â€” not "Creation is over-flourishing."

**Posture:** "Read these specific things"

#### The Direct Token Protocol

Unlike other modes where you ask open questions, Explore mode reads what you explicitly name. The tokens can be:

- **Nouns/Entities**: "my career", "this relationship", "the house", "my mother"
- **Verbs/Dynamics**: "the move", "leaving", "the decision", "starting over"

Each token gets mapped to a single draw, creating a diagnostic of multiple focal points simultaneously.

#### The Three-Layer Sentence

Each token reading follows a specific format:
```

```

This grounds the abstract in the concrete â€” YOUR named concern, expressed through a specific signature, located in a specific domain.

#### Explore Mode Constraints

- Describes what IS, not what will be
- Uses: "is expressing as", "is structured by", "is currently shaped through"
- Avoids: "will cause", "means that", "will lead to", "destiny"
- Each reading ends with actionable framing:
  - Imbalanced cards: "This is visible now. It is adjustable."
  - Balanced cards: "This is visible now. It is usable."

If you enter tokens in predictive language ("will the move work out?"), the system treats your framing as diagnostic information â€” it shows where your attention is magnetized, not where the future is fixed. The reading locates you in NOW, regardless of how you frame the question.
