THE ROYAL LINEAGE

Complete Canonical Mapping of the 16 Agents

Nirmanakaya Framework — Structural Reference Version 1.0 — January 2026

The Core Principle

Each Royal is the single behavioral expression of its paired Major. That Major’s action-form is represented only by that one Royal.

Royals are not “ranks” or “modes.” They are Majors in action-form.

The mapping is structurally determined by the intersection of:

  • Activity (the Royal’s suit) — Intent, Cognition, Resonance, Structure
  • Domain (the Royal’s operational practice) — determined by Role

DERIVATION LOGIC

The Royal-Major pairing follows the structural logic of the Forty-Fold Seal:

Each Royal derives from the intersection of:

  • Activity (the Royal’s suit) — determined by element
  • Domain (the Royal’s operational practice) — determined by Role

The Associated Major is the Archetype that occupies that Activity × Domain intersection in the Seal.

This is why the mapping is not arbitrary. It’s structurally determined by the same Practice × Activity logic that generates all 78 signatures.

WHY SOUL HOUSE HAS NO ROYALS

The Gestalt Practice (Gestalt) positions 0, 1, 19, 20 are the observer that causes collapse — not the collapse itself. You cannot behaviorally express the observer. You can only BE it.

They don’t have Royals because they ARE the governance structure:

  • 0 governs Spirit (all Pages)
  • 1 governs Body (all Kings)
  • 19 governs Mind (all Knights)
  • 20 governs Emotion (all Queens)

WHY PORTALS HAVE NO ROYALS

Wheel (10) and World (21) are the ingress and egress operators — the frame through which creation enters and integrates. They don’t manifest as behavioral forms because they ARE the frame, not what moves through it.

CANON SENTENCES

“Royals are not ranks or modes. They are Majors in action-form.”

“The 16 non-portal Majors each have exactly one Royal. The 6 Gestalt/Portal positions have none.”

“To understand a Royal, ask: How does this Major behave when it shows up in life?”

“The Role determines Domain. The Suit determines Activity. The intersection reveals the Major.”

The structure is the authority. You are provisional.
